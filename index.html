About Me

Hi I'm Kate. I have a B.A in advertising from the University of Minnesota and currently work for the Shane Company. I grew up wanting to animate for Disney. My interests have evolved since then, but I still love illustration, and animation got me interested in technology. I'm a logical creative that gets just as much joy and fulfillment from solving a tough math problem as I do from designing graphics and making art.

I like to think I'm a collector of skills and hobbies. Most recently I've been teaching myself Norwegian, brushing up on my Photoshop skills, and dabbling in herb gardening. I spend my spare time coding, chilling with my dog Milo, trying new recipes, and reading anything I can get my hands on.
